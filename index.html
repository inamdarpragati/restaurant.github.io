Fly Dining.

Fly Dining’, an all-new restaurant has recently opened up in Bengaluru that has everything you have ever dreamt of. With a stunning view, delectable cuisines, and a memorable flying experience, Fly Dining in Bengaluru will leave you spellbound. No doubt, this new attraction in Bengaluru is something you can’t afford to skip from your bucket list. The compelling concept of sky dining in India was first introduced in 2008 and finally the restaurant came to life after 10 years in Bengaluru. Fly dining in Bengaluru can accommodate 22 people at once wherein a staff of four people is seated in the centre to serve you. The outstanding menu consists of snacks, mocktails, grilled chicken and dessert. India’s first hanging restaurant in Bengaluru offers you a mocktail meal worth ₹3,999 per person which will last around 30 mins, and a proper 1 hour dinner at the price of ₹6,999 per person.
